The problem

You find out a job lost money weeks after it's done. By then, you've already bid the next one wrong.

QuickBooks wasn't built for job costing.

No CSI codes, no per-job P&L, no change order tracking. You end up with receipts in a shoebox and a spreadsheet 3 weeks behind.

Late data = bad bids.

When your bookkeeper reconciles 6 weeks later, you've already priced the next project on wrong numbers. That's how margins quietly shrink.

Enterprise ERP is overkill.

Procore and Sage are built for 50-person operations. If you run 3 crews, you need job costing without the six-figure contract.

Step 1

Upload invoices

PDFs, photos, forwarded emails — even blurry receipts. AI extracts vendor, amount, and line items automatically.

Step 2

Costs map to jobs

JobLedger learns your job numbers and cost codes. Every invoice maps to the right project. Ambiguous items get flagged, never silently misclassified.

Step 3

Entries post to QuickBooks

Review, approve, done. Job profitability stays current, and you bid the next one with real numbers.
